Our Mission

4 Pillars: Building Relationships, Meeting Needs, Volunteer Driven, PUSD Region
4CommunityCare is a 100% volunteer group providing food and other resources to local households in need within the Poway Unified School District.

How To Give

What items are needed? (please check expiration dates)

  • Flour and sugar
  • Rice
  • Cake, brownies, or muffin mixes
  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Peanut butter and jam
  • Pasta sauce (canned)
  • Cereal and oatmeal
  • Boxed macaroni and cheese
  • Soup (canned)
  • Chicken or tuna (canned)
  • Other non-perishables
  • Paper towels, kleenex tissues
  • Gift cards (grocery and gas)
  • Picked from your garden - fruits and vegetables

About 4CommunityCare

4CommunityCare (4CC) is a committed group of volunteers providing food and assistance to local seniors and families in the neighborhoods of Poway Unified School District. We partner with local nonprofits and churches to build strong community relationships that shape the way we serve.

4CommunityCare has a number of like-minded volunteers that previously worked together many years with another agency that disbanded in August 2020, called Friends & Family Community Connection (FFCC).  

Without a pause, the work to help families with food resources (which intensified due to the pandemic), has gone forward.  After developing relationships with families, we often learn other ways we can provide assistance.  Gift cards, clothing and other household items are passed directly to local families who struggle with daily expenses.
